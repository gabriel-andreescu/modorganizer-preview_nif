[size=4][color=#00ffff]Compatibility[/color][/size]
Use the download that matches your Mod Organizer version:

[list]
[*]MO2 2.5.2: NIF Preview MO2-2.5.2[/*]
[*]MO2 2.5.3beta11: NIF Preview MO2-2.5.3beta11[/*]
[/list]

Versions older than MO2 2.5.2 are not supported because they do not provide BSA preview support for plugins.

Do not mix the two downloads. MO2 2.5.2 and MO2 2.5.3beta11 use different plugin ABIs.

[size=4][color=#00ffff]Original Description[/color][/size]
[quote][size=5]Overview[/size]
This plugin allows you to preview NIF files in Mod Organizer by double-clicking them. This gives you a more convenient way to quickly view a NIF file without having to open Nifskope in the VFS each time.

The preview will use loose textures from your enabled mods, or from vanilla game BSAs if applicable.

[size=5]Installation[/size]
Download the ZIP that matches your MO2 version, then extract it to the Mod Organizer plugins directory (<MO2 DIR>\plugins).[/quote]
